# Drag and Drop in `docling.js`

Drag and Drop is one of those functionalities that a lot of apps could use.

I have been thinking about it a lot myself, and the more I do, the more I realize it’s an interface pattern that could be of use in many of the kinds of workflows that we use. 

* classifying a list of talks
* sorting recorded words into tone groups
* dragging items onto tags (or vice versa)
* grouping items (like words, say) into semantic domains
* dragging clusters of grammatical features from one word to another (for instance, if they agree)
* sorting a messy fieldwork archive into a tree structure without screwing with the original files http://localhost:1111/languages/hiligaynon/backup-ucsb-fieldmethods/
