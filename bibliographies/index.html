Dear fellow linguist,

There’s a lot we can do together to make better use of the web platform in language documentation.

My goal is to work with linguists who are learning to navigate the maze of web technology, and help them understand the elegance, usability, and dynamic nature of its fundamental design — and maybe have some fun in the process.

The creation of durable digital representations of language is an ever-more urgent task: I hope we can work together.

Cheers!
— Pat
